Biography

Dr. Chaofeng Ye graduated from Michigan State University in the United States. In recent years, he has undertaken national and provincial-level projects such as the National Natural Science Foundation General Project and the National Natural Science Foundation Enterprise Innovation and Development Joint Fund Key Project.

He has been awarded the Shanghai University Distinguished Professor (Oriental Scholar), Pujiang Talent, and the Teaching Achievement Award at Tsinghua University. His entrepreneurial projects have received support from the National Innovation Center.
